HAT-S experiment on DIV2K dataset

Compare the results of 3 experiments on the HAT-S model

Visualize the results of 3 experiments on the HAT-S model. Information to be visualized includes PSNR, SSIM score statistics, representative SR images of Set5, Set14, BSD100, Manga109.

HAT-S original

The HAT-S model was reused without changing anything.

HAT-S applies Patch-Mosaic

Train on the DIV2K dataset with Patch-Mosaic data augmentation applied.

HAT-S applies Perceptual Loss

Use Perceptual Loss to replace L1Loss for model training (select conv2_2 block in VGG19 network).

HAT-S applies MixLosses Loss

Use MixLosses Loss, which is a custom loss combined from Perceptual Loss and L1 Loss.

Datasets

  • DIV2K: consisting of 800 images with different scenes for training models.
  • DIV2K-Patch-Mosaic: Includes DIV2K and an additional 200 images generated by Patch-Mosaic data augmentation method.
  • Set5, Set14, BSD100, Manga109: includes many images with different scenes used for model testing.

HAT-S experiment on custom dataset

Applicable to the Vietnam-Landscape dataset

The experiment uses a custom dataset of Vietnamese landscape images, using the HAT-S model pre-trained on the DIV2K dataset to continue training.

Applicable to the Human-Faces dataset

The experiment uses the HAT-S model pre-trained on the DIV2K dataset to continue training on the Human-Faces dataset. Aim to be able to denoise old photos, blurry photos, black and white photos.

Datasets

  • Vietnam-Landscape: includes 1000 HD images of landscapes and scenic spots in Vietnam. It is splitted to 900 for training and 100 for testing.
  • Human-Faces: includes color and black-and-white photos of human faces. It is splitted to nearly 3000 images for training and more than 300 images for testing.
